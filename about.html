Globe icon What to do! Globe icon

Minecraft is such a versatile game, whether you want to go to war or create art! There is so much to do! You can play online with your friends creating a world together, or join servers for free and compete in build challenges, hunger games and so much more...


Some very popular servers are:
Hypixel (IP= mc.hypixel.net),
The Hive (IP= play.hivemc.com) and Brawl (IP= mcsl.brawl.com).


If you want to find out more you should purchase Minecraft books! They have so many helpful tips and interesting information! Take a peak!
